Who's Antonio?

Tillandsia Antonio is an airplant with a bold personality. Sporting a stunning, multi-pink quill, this plant is ready to turn your living space into a tropical paradise. Give him a spot and watch the magic unfold.
MEET ANTONIO

The Perfect Spot for Antonio

 
Antonio thrives best in bright, indirect light, and moderate humidity. He's a versatile guy, perfect for hanging terrariums or just about any corner that needs a pop of color and character.
Did you know Antonio's a Tillandsia, a type of Bromeliad? These tropical beauties are epiphytes, which means they get most of their nutrients from the air. That's why we call Antonio an 'airplant.'
